Create a product schedule

A product schedule contains all the information needed about the products you want to export.

You can add products to a schedule one at a time or upload a spreadsheet to add them in bulk.

Any products included in kits need to be listed individually.

Before you start

Your answers must apply to every product on the schedule. You will need to create a new schedule for any product that has different details.

Product eligibility:

  • if the company is the product manufacturer
  • product safety requirements
  • brand name holder information
  • if any item is a raw material
  • country of manufacture

Add your products:

  • the legislation that applies to your product - ask your technical team or check your product Material Safety Data Sheet (MSDS) or Cosmetic Product Data Information Sheet if you are not sure
  • product name, spelled correctly
  • product use
