Create a product schedule

A product schedule contains all the information needed about the products you want to export.

You can add products to a schedule one at a time or upload a spreadsheet to add them in bulk.

Any products included in kits need to be listed individually.

Before you start

Your answers must apply to every product on the schedule. You will need to create a new schedule for any product or raw material that has different details.

You will need to know:

  • product names spelled correctly
  • product manufacturer details
  • the legislation that applies to your product - ask your technical team or check your product Material Safety Data Sheet (MSDS) if you are not sure
