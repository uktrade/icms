Do you need to export any of these products?

To export any of these products, you will need to visit the correct service.

An export certificate for:

  • medical devices - Medical and Healthcare Products Regulatory Agency (MHRA) and guidance on exporting medical devices (GOV.UK)
  • biocides - Health and Safety Executive (HSE)
  • medicines for human use - Medicines and Healthcare products Regulatory Agency (MHRA)
  • products that animals have contact with, such as veterinary medicines - Veterinary Medicines Directorate (GOV.UK) and guidance on export certificates for medicines for animals (GOV.UK)
  • foods, drinks and supplements - Rural Payments Agency (RPA) and guidance on the food standards agency
  • pesticides on free sale in the UK - Health and Safety Executive (HSE)

Visit SPIRE through Department for Business and Trade if you need an export licence for:

  • security items
  • military and civilian use firearms
  • police and paramilitary items
  • radioactive products
  • classified information

For all other export products, visit Department for Business and Trade guidance.
