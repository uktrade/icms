Apply for an export certificate

Use this service to apply for a certificate for use in overseas markets to provide assurance on specific products.

Certificate of Free Sale (CFS)

Products which meet UK standards that fall under Department for Business and Trade legislation.

Good Manufacturing Practice Certificate (GMPC)

Cosmetic products which meet UK good manufacturing practice standards. For use in China only.

Certificate of Manufacture (CoM)

Pesticides that are solely for use in overseas markets and will not be placed on the UK market.

You will be taken to GOV.UK One Login. You will need to sign in or create an account before you can apply.
